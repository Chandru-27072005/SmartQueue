FEATURES

Why Smart Queue?

A simple and efficient solution for customers and service providers to reduce waiting confusion.

Online Token Booking

Users can book tokens online without standing in a physical queue.

Estimated Waiting Time

Customers can know approximate waiting time before their turn.

Live Queue Tracking

See current token number, people ahead and queue progress in real time.

Admin Dashboard

Admins can call next token, mark completed and manage daily bookings easily.

Crowd Level Indicator

Shows crowd level as Low, Medium or High for better time planning.

Better Customer Experience

Reduces waiting stress and improves service center organization.
